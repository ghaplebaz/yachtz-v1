Overview

A tri-deck flagship of quiet power

The Grande 36M is Azimut's carbon-tech flagship — a 36-metre tri-deck that pairs ocean-crossing capability with the proportions of a private residence. Floor-to-ceiling glass, a beach club at the waterline, and an owner's deck that spans the full beam.
